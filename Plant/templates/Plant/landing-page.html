PLANT CARE

A web-app for taking care of your plants.



What is this about?

Our app provides an IoT based solution for taking care of your plants. It feeds information about your plant/s and its surrounding environment, and then that data is analysed and proper actions are taken. With this Automatic Plant Watering System, you will never have to worry about your plants going thirsty again!


Features

Mentioned below are the main features of our project.

An IoT based Solution

Efficient use of IoT components such as sensors, actuators, micro-controllers.

Automated Reactions

Data such as soil humidity, rainfall are analysed and based on that AUTOMATIC watering of plant is done.

FIT it and FORGET it System.

Once done with set-up, you can go freely to vacations without worrying about your plants' health.

ALERT system

Gives appropiate alerts about resources such as water supply, actuautor states. A SMS alert is sent if water supply is low.

Cloud based System.

Real time monitoring is done and sensors data are displayed on cloud based system.

Gives your Plant a VOICE

It provides a way to communicate between you and your plants.
